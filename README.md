## What is a Stargate?

A Stargate is a sophisticated ancient device that enables instant travel between distant locations across the universe,
originating from [the science fiction franchise: Stargate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stargate).

In this mod, you will be able to find and build such devices to travel nearly instantly to other gates and explore new
dimensions

## How does it work?

A Stargate functions like a sophisticated phone system: each gate has a unique address, and you can dial other gates
using a special keypad to establish a connection.

There are 3 different gates in this mod:

- **Milky Way**: The classic orange gate.
- **Pegasus**: The more refined blue gate.
- **Universe**: The rusty old black gate.

### Stargate Addresses

Every gate has a unique address composed of 9 symbols arranged in a specific pattern:

- **Positions 1-6**: Represent the Stargate's region on the XZ plane (spatial coordinates)
- **Position 7**: Indicates the dimension where the gate is located and allows contact with gates of different types
- **Position 8**: Provides a more refined location within the Stargate region. This is useful when multiple gates exist
  in the same region, allowing you to choose which specific gate to contact
- **Position 9**: The point of origin symbol. This indicates that you have finished entering an address. Every gate
  address ends with this same symbol
-

> **Tip**: You can dial with fewer than 9 symbols!
>
> If you want to contact a gate in the same dimension and of the same type as yours, you can enter just the first 6
> symbols, then press the point of origin symbol as position 7 to complete the dial.

### Obtaining Gate Addresses

To discover a gate's address, right-click on it with a piece of paper. This will create an address card showing all
possible addresses for that gate.

<img alt="Stargate Address Card" src="./img/address_card.png" style="padding: 8px; max-width: 600px">

*An address card displaying the gate's coordinates*

Each of the 3 gate types has its own unique symbol set. When dialing, you must use the symbols corresponding to your
source gate type.

### Dialing Home Device (DHD)

To enter addresses and dial gates, you will use a Dialing Home Device (DHD). Currently, two variants of DHDs exist, and
both are compatible with all gate types.

## How do I find or build a gate?

> **Future Update**: Gates will spawn naturally and rarely in the Overworld.

### How to refine Naquadah

To craft a gate, you will first need to find a rare resource called ***Naquadah***.

Naquadah is a rare, super-heavy, and extremely dense chemical element used in most technology in this mod. It can also
be used as a powerful fuel for furnaces and blast furnaces.

> **Future Update**: Naquadah will be a resource that only spawns in another dimension.
> For now, it's available through a special crafting recipe.

#### Obtaining Naquadah

<img alt="Naquadah crafting recipe" src="./img/recipes/naquadah.png" height="200" style="padding: 8px; max-width: 600px">

#### Processing Naquadah into alloy

**Step 1**: Crush the naquadah in a trommel

> Note: Due to Naquadah's extreme density as a heavy chemical element, the trommel has a 20% chance of yielding nothing.

<img alt="Naquadah dust recipe from trommel" src="./img/recipes/naquadah_dust.png" style="padding: 8px; max-width: 600px">

**Step 2**: Mix the powder with crude steel

<img alt="Naquadah crude alloy recipe" src="./img/recipes/naquadah_crude_alloy.png" height="200" style="padding: 8px; max-width: 600px">

**Step 3**: Smelt the crude alloy in a blast furnace

### Building Your Stargate

With all the materials ready, you need to assemble them in a 7x7 ring structure.

> **Note**: Stargates can be built vertically or horizontally on the floor.

#### Construction Guides

- 9 Chevrons
- 1 Core
- 6 Ring Blocks
